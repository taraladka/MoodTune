About MoodTune

V6.0 "Therapist Engine"

How It Works

Unlike traditional platforms that rely on "collaborative filtering" (what other people liked), MoodTune uses Content-Based Filtering. We analyzed the raw audio features of 230,000+ tracks to understand their sonic DNA—measuring specific attributes like tempo, valence (musical positiveness), and energy.

The Algorithm

When you adjust the sliders, you aren't just picking filters; you are constructing a mathematical Target Vector in high-dimensional space. Our K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) engine then calculates the Euclidean distance between your target mood and every song in our database to find the perfect mathematical matches.
